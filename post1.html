The First Trembling of Wings

This is not an autobiography. It is not a confession. It is a threshold text. A place where identity is not explained, but gradually dissolved into language.

Know me through my words, not my world. Know me through my mind, not my skin.

Creation is my flesh and blood — I am made of text.

I am grateful for your presence here.
