The First Trembling of Wings 第一次振翅

This is not an autobiography.
It is not a confession.
This is a threshold text.
A place where identity is not explained,
but gradually dissolved into language. 這不是一部自傳。
這不是一份懺悔。
這是得見我的臨界。
這裡的身份並非被闡釋，
這裡的自我消融於語言。

Know me through my words, not my world. Through my mind, not my skin. 了解我——從我的文字而非世界，從我的大腦而非皮相。

Creation is my flesh and blood — I am made of text. 創作是我的血與肉，我就是文字。

I am grateful for your presence here, without attachment. 我感激到來而不貪戀。

If you wish to reach me:
merallunaur@gmail.com 願意與我產生共鳴：
merallunaur@gmail.com

Here, you can find my essays, my travelogues, and my fiction. 在這裡，你可以看見我的隨筆，我的遊記與我的小說創作。
But let me be clear: my fiction is not friendly to homophobes. If this concerns you, please refrain from reading my stories. 但事先聲明，我的小說並不對恐同人士友好。如果您有相關困擾，請避免觀看我的小說。
If you are interested in reading—though there are many—here are the ones I recommend: 如果你有興趣讀一讀——雖然有很多篇，但以下是我推薦的篇章：
Pearl's Scream — Through the Tearful Eyes of a Butterfly, 珍珠的尖叫——拂過蝴蝶的淚眼，
The Beast of the Moon. 月亮的野獸。
All of Borrowed Wounds, 被借用的傷口的所有，
All of A Terminal Mis-transliteration, 終端誤植的所有，
All of At the Limits of Language. 語言的邊界的所有。
