Afterword 後記

For those France x England shippers who have lost the ability to appreciate, I am at a height that neither ordinary fast-food writers nor readers can reach or comprehend. 對於已經失去欣賞能力的仏英人來說，我是無論一般快餐文手還是讀者都不可企及和理解的高度。
What distinguishes us from machines is that we think. But time and again, the breaking of boundaries tells me that some people are inferior even to machines—they are uncivilised swine. 我們與機械的區別在於我們會思考。但一次一次的下限突破告訴我，有的人不如甚至機械，而是未開化的豬玀。
Clearly, the theme of "Terminal Misentry" is not merely love, yet all they can see is love. After repeatedly quantum-speed-reading my work, they toss out comments devoid of substance or understanding, trying to flatter me. I am not your fashion accessory to flaunt your niche hobbies. It is you who need me to add distinction to your laughably ordinary lives. 明明《終端誤植》的主題不只是愛情卻只能看見愛情，數次通過量子速讀後毫無營養與理解的評論試圖討好我，我不是你們彰顯自己小眾愛好的時尚單品。是你們需要我來為自己平凡得可笑的人生增加辨識度。
Humanity still contracts new diseases: impatience, stupidity, arrogance, and conceit. 人類依舊會得新的病症：浮躁、愚蠢、自以為是與傲慢。
Thank you to Ms. No Nickname, who truly understood my writing. 感謝讀懂我文章的沒暱稱女士。
Thank you to all my online supporters and real-life friends. 感謝支持我的所有網友和現生朋友們。
Thank you to myself. 感謝我自己。
Your new world ended the moment you looked down on me. 你們的新世界在輕視我的那一刻便已終結。
You say you forgive my arrogance. Unfortunately, I have nothing to regret, and I refuse to apologise. 居然說會原諒我的傲慢。可惜我沒有什麼可後悔的，也拒絕道歉。
