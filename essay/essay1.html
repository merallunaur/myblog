A World of Pure Imagination 純粹的想象世界

The first time I published a book took me four years; this time, it took two. If the pride of being a creator doesn't bring me negativity and pain, that would be the best thing, in my view. 第一次出書用了我四年，這一次用了兩年。如果身為創作者的自尊不會給我消極和痛苦，在我看來是最好的事。

I once refused everyone, including all those who had helped me. I went a long time without creating, but every time I picked up a pen, I deeply felt the fortune of being able to create. Over the past few years, I have gained more confidence in my abilities and strength. Now I no longer need anyone's approval. No longer do I need the shelter of布拉吉 or the followers from中庭. Because I, who have given both my love and my life, am my own one and only god. 我曾拒絕所有人，包括曾經幫助過我的所有人。我有很久沒有創作，但每次拾起筆都深感能創作的幸運。幾年來，我對於自己的能力及力量更加有信念。現在我已經不需要任何人認可我。不再需要什麼布拉吉的庇護或來自中庭的擁躉。因為把愛與命一同給出的我就是我自己的唯一神明。
I remember when I took the wand test on the Harry Potter official website, there was a question asking which of my qualities I was most proud of. I chose imagination. I am grateful that my natural disposition allows me to derive pure joy from the world of imagination without needing external feedback. Apart from South Korea, I have never been to any other country—even within my own country, my travels have been limited to the eastern and southern regions. Yet my imagination has enabled me to bear witness to certain fictions, allowing my words to endow fictionality with reality and create those dreamlike worlds. 記得在哈利波特官網做魔杖測試時曾有一題問我最為自己的什麼品質而驕傲。我選擇了想像力。我很慶幸，我天生的性格使我不需要反饋，也能從想像力的世界裡獲得純粹的樂趣。除了韓國之外，我從未去過其他國家，甚至國內也僅限於東部南部的旅遊。但我的想像力卻使我得以成為一部分虛構性的見證者，讓我的文字賦予虛構性現實，創造那些夢境般的世界。
"Come with me, and you will see a world composed of pure imagination." 「跟我來吧，你會看到純粹想像力構成的世界。」
I have always believed that flattening characters—confining them to bickering, sweet talk, lust, and quarrels—is a shallow and arrogant act. A person's life is as resplendent and ever-changing as a kaleidoscope. Therefore, love that transcends language and can be felt in the instant of eye contact is a far more powerful, enduring, and profound existence. Unlike my previous work, which pursued a fervent, intense, and bloody kind of love, I have come to understand that the mystery and power of love are not limited to destructiveness, nor does the beauty of love lie solely in the growth or insight gained through its violence and pain. Love, as something learned, is about using gentleness and kindness to understand and support others, to melt, reshape, and piece together a wounded heart. And gentleness and kindness are the virtues I most lack and most admire. 我一向認為，把角色平面化，局限於鬥嘴、情話、愛欲和爭吵，是一種淺薄而狂妄的表現。人的生命如萬花筒般絢爛、千變萬化。因此，超越語言的理解，眼神交匯的瞬間就能讓人感覺到的愛意，是更為強大、持久而深刻的存在。與我上一部無料不同，我曾追求熾熱的、強烈的、血腥的愛意。但是愛的奧秘與強大並不止於毀滅性，而愛的美好之處也不僅僅在於它的暴力和痛苦所給予人的成長或感悟，愛的後天習得，是以溫柔善良，理解並支撐他人，融化、重塑並拼湊某顆傷痕累累的心。而溫柔善良，是我最欠缺，也最羨慕的美德。
I am forever grateful to Francis and Arthur. Thank you for maintaining gentleness and steadfastness in favorable times, and for unleashing strength and resilience in adversity. Thank you for always inspiring and enlightening me. 我永遠感謝弗朗西斯和亞瑟。謝謝你們在順境中保持的溫柔而堅定和在逆境中迸發的力量與不屈。謝謝你們一直激勵並啟示著我。
As long as my mind is still accompanied by imagination, unobscured by fog, I will not stop creating. 只要我的腦子依舊伴生著想像，不被霧氣所遮蔽，我就不會停止創作。
I hope that everyone has countless beautiful and clear memories, and a strong and kind heart. 希望所有人有無數美好而清晰的回憶，有一顆堅強而善良的心。
