Homelessness 無鄉

One evening, I realised that my memories of Seoul were slowly fading away. Everyone knows that the loss of memory is a natural law that cannot be resisted, just like deciduous trees become bare in winter. In the final cycle of a person's life—the last winter—will only withered, fragile memories remain? I had prepared myself early on for leaving Seoul after graduation, so every time I walked the street from school to the subway station, I told myself: "This is a place I need to remember." It started with a few stray cats near my home. At first, I approached these reachable anchors cautiously—warm, fuzzy things. But my mind became like a giant stomach that could never be filled with memories. Until, in those final months, I devoured Heukseok-dong like gulping down a bowl of porridge. Perhaps this gluttony was my sin, and the punishment it ultimately gave me was an endless hunger. 故鄉。
Between the school and the university hospital, there is a shop selling cute little things—animal-shaped phone cases, cups, and so on. I've always loved small animals, which is probably why I went in a couple of times. But on the night I became aware of my own sin, when I once again immersed myself in boundless memories and traced back along that street, I couldn't be sure whether it was there or not. It was like a firefly resting in my palm suddenly losing its glow. In a panic, I tried to recall other details. The memories that had once guarded me like silent sentinels no longer answered my calls. 某個晚上，我發現我對首爾的記憶正在漸漸消逝。所有人都知道記憶流失是無法對抗的自然法則，就像落葉喬木在冬天會變得光禿禿的。人生命的最後週期，最後一個寒冬，是不是也只剩枯黃而脆弱的記憶了呢？我很早就做好了畢業將要離開首爾的準備，於是每次走過學校到地鐵站的那條街道，我都告訴自己:「這是我將要記住的地方。」從家附近的幾隻流浪貓開始，一開始我小心翼翼地接近這些觸手可得的錨點。帶有溫度的，毛茸茸的。但我的腦子彷彿變成了一個巨大的胃，無法被記憶填滿。直到最後數月間，我像狼吞虎嚥一碗粥那般，痛飲著黑石洞。或許是這樣暴食的罪過，最終給我的刑罰是永無止境的飢餓吧。
Unlike Beijing, which I tried desperately to escape, my feelings for Seoul were completely different—as if this was the place where I was born for the first time. A part of my self was shaped in Seoul, and thus remained there. After I received my Alien Registration Card in Seoul, I started smoking. Women's cigarettes in Seoul contain mint, giving me an unprecedented clarity of mind. At first, one cigarette a day, then three, then half a pack a day. I smoke not because I like it, but because it is only in harming my own body, only when my body can feel something, that I regain a sense of control over it. In my twenty-some years of walking, only pain and fear have been eternal. Whenever I feel happy, an inexplicable fear strikes me again. At the same time, I feel guilty for this inexplicable emotion. Did I do it on purpose? Did I lure this emotion into my life and develop a pathological relationship with it? Did I force myself to become a victim, as if that would justify my indifference? I cannot know for certain. Just as I cannot find comfort in memories, all my emotions float in the air like bubbles, bursting at the slightest touch. 學校和學校附屬病院之間有一家賣可愛小玩意的店——小動物造型的耳機殼和水杯什麼的。我一直喜歡各種小動物，可能也因此走進去過兩次。而我察覺到自身罪過的那個晚上，當我再次置身於無邊際的回憶，再溯那條街道，我卻無法確定它在不在哪裡。彷彿一隻停在我掌心的螢火蟲突然失去了光彩。懷著極度的恐慌，我回憶起其他細節。曾經如沉默的護衛般守護著我的那些回憶，不再回應我的呼喚。
Those who cannot bear farewells cannot recall the past either. Some people take memories as strength, like chewing a piece of chocolate. But for me, it has never been that way. I cannot extract anything from memories except bitterness. My process of recollection is like looking through tangled waterweed at a school of undead goldfish. The colours of their scales remain in my memory. Fish never close their eyes; perhaps death is like sleep. But I remember every moment of their swimming, so I cannot accept these motionless dead things now. 不同於我拼了命也要逃離的北京，我對首爾的感情很不一樣——彷彿是第一次完整出生的地方。我的一部分自我在首爾被塑造，也就因此留在那個地方。在首爾拿到登錄證後，我開始吸菸。首爾的女士煙裡加了薄荷，使人腦子前所未有的清晰。一開始一天一根，然後是一天三根，最後一天半包。我吸菸，不是因為我喜歡吸，而是因為在對自己身體的傷害中，或是當我的身體能感受到什麼的時候，我才能找回對身體的掌控權。在我二十多年的行走中，只有痛苦和恐懼是永恆的。每當我感到愉快，沒來由的恐懼就又會擊中我。同時，我因這種沒來由的感情而愧疚。是我故意的嗎？是我引誘這種情緒來到我的生活，並與它發展出病態的戀愛嗎？是我硬要把自己塑造成一個受害者，彷彿這樣合理化我的冷漠嗎？我無法確切知道。就像我無法從回憶中得到安慰那般，我的所有感情都像泡沫般飄在空中，一碰就炸。
Seoul is not my hometown. When I left home for the first time at eighteen to live on my own, that place I left was not my hometown either. Hometown is always embodied in people—parents, relatives, friends... But my parents are also strangers to the place where I was born. My mother left Sichuan to work in third-line mining construction. My father was assigned to work there. They cannot speak the local dialect, and neither can I. The feeling of being adrift seems to have been passed down to me through my blood and umbilical cord. Objectively speaking, my parents are very tolerant, which is why they raised a selfish daughter. I was raised by my grandmother, who cannot speak Mandarin, so that place was not her hometown either. I never had my own room before I was eighteen, not even my own bed. Every night, I slept with my grandmother. She always told me to sleep close to her; if I didn't, she would say I looked down on her for being from the countryside. As a child, I couldn't handle this burden; I just thought I wasn't mature enough. Every time I pressed close to her, her scent, her aged voice speaking in dialect, only made me feel the approach of death ever more clearly. 無法承受告別的人，也就無法回憶過去。有人會將回憶當做力量，就像嚼一塊巧克力。可對我從來不是這樣，我無法從回憶裡汲取除了悲涼之外的任何東西。我的回憶過程是透過交纏的水草看一群不褪色的死金魚。鱗片的顏色仍舊在我的記憶裡。魚不會閉眼，也許死就像睡覺。但我記得他們游動的每個時刻，也就無法接受如今一動不動的死物。
Even now, I don't like meeting my parents. I know my grandmother expects me to visit, yet I don't want to go to her. My grandmother now lives in Guangzhou with my aunt's family—a place even farther from her hometown. Visiting her means staying at my aunt's house, sleeping in the same bed as my grandmother, listening to her endlessly talk about the times I lost my temper, and how unfilial the other grandchildren are. I cannot bear this guilt and overwhelming emotion. I want to leave. 首爾不是我的故鄉。我十八歲第一次離開家自己生活。那個離開的地方也不是我的故鄉。故鄉總是寄託在人身上的，父母，親戚，朋友……但我的父母對於我出生的那個地方來說，也都是異鄉人。我母親跟隨三線採礦業建設離開了四川。我父親被分配到那裡工作。他們不會說那個地方的方言，我也不會。漂泊感彷彿跟隨血液與臍帶傳到了我的身體裡。客觀來說我的父母十分包容，因此養出自私的女兒。我是我奶奶帶大的，她不會說普通話，因此，那裡也不是她的故鄉。我在18歲之前沒有自己的房間，甚至沒有自己的床。每個晚上我都跟奶奶睡在一起。她總叫我貼著她睡，不貼著就會說我嫌棄她來自農村。小小的我無法處理這種負擔，只當自己不夠懂事。每次貼近奶奶，她身上的味道，她說著方言時蒼老的語氣，只讓我感到越來越近的死亡。
Let me talk about "there." Since birth, I have been experiencing loss of control. In high school, I had my first love, and in the end, that man raped me. I don't even know if I can define it that way—because I didn't want to, I was afraid. But what I showed was compliance and affection. My love, conflated with sex, drove me to bind others with my body, even though the outcome of my first love was still that he cheated on me—because no one truly stops for my soul. If anyone does, they must be lying. Those who stop for a momentary interest, like, or because they sense my fragility or niche appeal—I suspect they are only prying into my privacy. I am certain they will leave after seeing through me completely and tell my story to others as a joke. I ruin every relationship, hurt others, hurt each other. I am even more afraid that I am seeking parental care from partners, using excessive demands and emotional blackmail to trap him, testing him with countless hysterical outbursts to see if he will leave—just as some parents do. 直到現在我也不喜歡跟父母見面，明明知道奶奶期待我去看她，我卻不想到她身邊去。奶奶現在和姑姑一家住在廣州，那是一個離她的故鄉更遠的地方。去看她意味著住在姑姑家，而住在別人家裡，跟奶奶睡在一張床上，聽她永恆地說起我情緒失控發火的時候，以及其他孫輩不孝的時候。我無法承受這種愧疚與巨大的情緒。我想走。
Use a diamond to describe my heart—I am hard enough to hurt everyone. But if you strike it in a certain direction, my heart shatters completely. I have always remembered in a way that cannot say goodbye. Perhaps I fear farewells, so I rehearse them countless times in advance, but the rehearsal itself is a repeated experience of loss. 再說說「那裡」。從出生時，我就一直在體驗失控。高中時我談了我的初戀，最終被那個男人強暴。我甚至不知道能不能定義成這樣 因為我不願意，我害怕。但我表現出來的是迎合與喜愛。與性混為一談的我的愛意，會促使我用身體捆綁別人，即使初戀的結局依舊是他出軌了別人——因為沒有人真正會為我的靈魂停留。如果有，那一定是在騙我。因為一時的興趣，喜歡，或嗅到了我的脆弱或小眾性而停留的人，我會覺得對方在窺探我的隱私。我認定對方會在看透我的一切後離開，並把我的事情當成笑話說給人聽。搞砸每一段關係，傷害他人，互相傷害。我更害怕自己在伴侶上找的是一種parental care，用這種過度索取和情緒綁架他，用無數次的歇斯底里試探他會不會離開——就像有些父母會做的那樣。
I know that besides leaving Seoul, I will also leave Hong Kong, leave Guangzhou, leave Shenzhen... I want to leave China, to live freely in a place where no one knows me. But I cannot bear my family's longing for me. My good and my evil are both impure. I have always thought that my family was stopping me from flying high, but in fact, it is myself. 用鑽石形容我的心吧，我是會讓每個人受傷的堅硬。但如果你按某個方向敲擊我的心，我的心就悉數崩裂。我一直用一種無法告別的方式去記憶。或許害怕告別，所以提前做無數次預演，但預演本身就是在反覆體驗失去。
I know that besides leaving Seoul, I will also leave Hong Kong, leave Guangzhou, leave Shenzhen... I want to leave China, to live freely in a place where no one knows me. But I cannot bear my family's longing for me. My good and my evil are both impure. I have always thought that my family was stopping me from flying high, but in fact, it is myself. 我知道除了離開首爾，我還會離開香港，離開廣州，離開深圳……我想離開中國，在沒人認識我的地方自由地生活。但我又無法承受家人對我的思念。我的善與惡都不夠純粹。我一直以為家人在阻止我高飛，實際上明明是我自己。
